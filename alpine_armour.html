Alpine Armour Marketing Promotional Event

Web UI Design, Marketing

Project Overview

Our group created fictional companies: Alpine Armour, an outdoor apparel company, and Marmot Media, a marketing agency. We took over the roles in Marmot Media, where our group was commissioned to lead a promotion event for Alpine Armour's new "Alta Jacket" release. The campaign focused on a Black Friday launch with a ski workshop giveaway, leveraging social media and the Alpine Armour website to gain brand awareness throughout the submission phase. The primary goals of the campaign was to boost brand awareness, drive sales, and grow the Alpine Armour community.

Research & Development

Jayden and I came up with the bare bones of the project, however, some refinements were made to suit instructor feedback. Before any planning of any event we need to come up with the goals and objectives of the campaign. Doing research we came up with.

Goals & Objectives
Increase Brand Awareness by 25% through Alpine Armour Social Networks

As the most obvious goal in an apparel company is to boost their sales in each quarter. We decided it was best to increase brand awareness instead because...

  • Customer Retention - Storytelling is a powerful tool for transforming potential customers into loyal ones.By consistently sharing Alpine Armour's commitment to sustainable practices, we can build a strong brand reputation that resonates with environmentally conscious consumers and fosters a dedicated community of Alpine Armour enthusiasts.
  • Reputation Management - Strong brands have a greater ability to rebuild their reputation when setbacks occur. Take Toyota, for example. Despite the 2023 recall of their Tundra and Tacoma trucks due to a rear axle manufacturing defect, the brand's reputation for reliability remained largely intact. By swiftly issuing a recall and offering exceptional customer service, Toyota reinforced its commitment to quality and safety, preserving the trust and loyalty of its customers.
Boost Alpine Armour Sales by 25% during the giveaway period.

Back to the obvious goal of boosting sales. I've come up with a way to increase the sales of the jacket

  • Pre Orders of the Jacket will increase the amount of submissions compared to the actual release date of the jacket.

After establishing our goals and objectives we set out to refine the workings of the event. Doing a competitive analysis of dominate figures in the outdoor apparel marketing. We were both inspiration from Arc'teryx's Backcountry Academy. We figured that instead of heavy focus on advanced skiers we choose to target the everyday individuals.

The reasoning being, is that targeting to a larger demographic will provide a more broader potential customer base as it correlates to word-of-mouth marketing as it will satisfy our goal of boosting brand awareness.

Timing

From knowledge and research, we determined that the best time to release a product is during the holiday season due to the trend of increased spending. Therefore, we decided to release the new jacket on Black Friday.

But you're probably asking when the giveaway will start and end. We came up with the dates from September 15 to November 30. This will allow about two and a half months for entry submissions and pre-orders for the new Alta Jacket. This will not only allow us time to increase brand awareness but also boost sales before the jacket even releases.

Target Market

Alpine Armour being an outdoor apparel company based in Vancouver we choice to focus our target audience on the everyday outdoor enthusiasts, urban adventurers, and those who are looking to be environmentally conscious.

Outdoor enthusiasts and casual weekend warriors are prime targets for the Alta jacket due to their active lifestyles and high praise for high-quality outdoor gear. Word-of-mouth marketing is a powerful tool for reaching specific niches within the demographic, and building trust within these groups is essential for a successful campaign.

Given the concern about climate change, environmentally conscious consumers are going to appreciate the Alta jacket's use of 95% recycled materials.

Target Region

Our target area is the Pacific Northwest, where consumers experience diverse weather conditions, including rain, snow, and wind.

Buying locally can:

  • Reduce shipping time and cost (fictional warehouse is in BC)
  • Lower the environmental carbon footprint due to shorter shipping distances
  • Support the local provincial economy
Location/Venue

The ski workshop will be located in Whistler, BC, due to its ideal conditions for filming and photography, as well as challenging conditions to put the Alta Jacket to the test.

Innovative UX/UI solutions

As the UI Designer of the group I knew that with big competitors in the outdoor apparel scene. I had to come up with schemes to add leverage for customers to come and visit the website.

A competitive analysis needed to be done on other brands as they each of these brands has its own unique selling point

Arc'teryx heavily emphasizes design and photography in their media to showcase product quality.

Patagonia known for its sustainable practices and encourages consumers to reduce consumption by offering a repair program.

The North Face, leverages celebrity endorsements For example, Ariana Grande's endorsement of the iconic North Face puffer led to a surge in jacket sales.

Implementations to add on the Alpine Armour Site:

  • Premium Visuals: High-quality photography will elevate our brand image and create a luxurious online experience. (inspiration from Arc'teryx)
  • Sustainability Commitment: Prioritizing sustainable practices, reducing environmental impact and ensuring long-lasting product durability. (inspiration from Patagonia)
  • Celebrity Endorsement: Ryan Reynolds' endorsement will boost brand visibility and attract a wider audience. (inspiration from The North Face)

Phases Of Marketing Event In-depth

Overview

Marmot Media will host a giveaway event to promote Alpine Armour's new "Alta Jacket" on Black Friday weekend. The grand prize is an all-expenses-paid ski workshop for four, complete with new mountain equipment and Alpine Armour apparel. Additionally, Ryan Reynolds and an Olympic skier will co-host a ski workshop in Whistler to demonstrate the jacket's capabilities in challenging conditions. The giveaway contest will run from September 15 to November 30. It will benefit Alpine Armour in three ways: increased brand awareness, boosted Alta Jacket sales, and expanded email newsletter subscribers. Ryan Reynolds will also promote the event on his social media channels. Contestants can enter the giveaway on social media and the Alpine Armour website. On social media, they can like, tag, and share posts to earn entries. On the website, they can sign up for the email newsletter and create an account to receive additional entries. We aim to achieve 1,000 shares, 5,000 likes, and 2,500 tagged friends on Instagram, as well as 2,500 new account website sign-ups. Our sales goals are a 25% increase during the giveaway period and a 50% increase on Black Friday weekend.

Phase 1 - Draw Submissions

Date: September 15, 2024 - November 30, 2024

Goal: Promote the announcement of the winners for Ski Trip Draw.

Objectives:

  • Receive over 1,500 new account sign ups with opt-in email allowance during the draw period.
  • 2,000 new single opt-in email sign ups.
  • At least 1,00 shares, 5,000 likes, and 2,500 tagged friends across the Instagram posts.
  • At least 1,000 visits from the Youtube via the link in description.
  • Increase Alta Jacket sales by 25% during draw submission & 50% during Black Friday Weekend.

Implementations:

  • Website is updated to show promotional banners highlighting the Alta Jacket and the event draw details.
  • Easily shareable Instagram posts to promote the draw and details on how to sign up to enter.
  • Youtube video released on the day of the announcement showing off the jacket and the community around the new jacket.
Phase 2 - Unveil Of Winners

Date: December 1, 2024

Goal: Promote the announcement of the winners for Ski Trip Draw.

Implementations:

  • The website will revert back to normal branding.
  • Instagram posts will announce the winners and encourage others to check out the website for more news.
Phase 3 - Event Weekend

Date: January 17 - January 20, 2025

Goal: Create promotional content using photos/video taken during the event.

Implementations:

  • Use content created during the event to promote the Alta jacket and encourage users to join the Alpine Armour community.
  • After the event, post a short recap Instagram post and stories.
  • Have Ryan Reynolds recap about event on our instagram.
Phase 4 - Post Event

Date: January 20 - February 15, 2025

Goal: Grow the Alpine Armour community following by showing the success of the event in social media posts and a website banner.

Implementations:

  • Use content from the Ski Trip to promote the success of the event and encourage users to sign up for the newsletter in order to hear about the next giveaway draw.
  • This will involve Instagram posts and a recap Youtube video.
