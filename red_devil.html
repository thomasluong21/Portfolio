Narrative

(Picture taken from PowerColor Website - PowerColor Product Page)

Upon receiving the project, I had just acquired a new Graphics Card (PowerColor Rx 7800xt Red Devil Edition). The original package design lacked a demonic or hellish presence, in my opinion.. I aimed to create something that truly represents the power of the dark side by fully unleashing the devil's wrath.

Typography

Satan Background Box Mock Up Ad

Opened and Sealed Box Mock Up

Front Box View

To emphasize the demonic theme, I chose a spooky font called 'Chiller' that evokes a sense of fear and bloodcurdling dread.

Inspired by demonic imagery, I thought it fitting to use the phrase "Unleash the Demons" to represent the full power of the graphics card. I also incorporated the Metallica-inspired font "Metal Lord" to enhance the overall theme.

Pieces to the Puzzle

My goal was to combine different elements that could represent the hellish atmosphere the product should have. I sought to use dark colors, sharp angles, and a dominant color scheme of black and red.

Focusing on the design process, I learned that simplicity in graphic design can significantly enhance a viewer's emotional response. Rather than overwhelm the audience with excessive elements, a well-crafted piece allows for effective communication.

Conclusion

In conclusion, I believe I accomplished my goal of redesigning the PowerColor Red Devil graphics card package to emphasize its dark, demonic presence. I incorporated typography that effectively represents the brand while creating an emotional response from the audience. I learned that the strength of design lies in the balance between chaos and simplicity, ensuring that every element serves a purpose and evokes a specific feeling.
