Narrative

(Picutre taken from PowerColor Website - https://www.powercolor.com/product?id=1692094158

At the time of receiving the project. I recently just got a new Graphics Card (PowerColor Rx 7800xt Red Devil Edition). Feeling a lack of the Demonic and Hellish presence in the theme of the original package design. I set to create something that represents the power of the dark side by fully unleashing the devil's wraith.

Typography

Satan's Box Mock Up Ad

Opened and Sealed Box Mock Up

Front View Box

To emphasize the demonic theme, I chose a spooky font called 'Chiller Font' that evokes a sense of fear and blood writhing.

Inspired by demonic imagery, I thought it would be fitting to use the phrase "Unleash the Demons" to represent the full power of the graphics card. I also incorporated the Metallica-inspired font "Metal Lord" to enhance the overall theme.

Pieces to the puzzle

The other elements that make up this package design

  • light smokey background
  • Image traced of the actual Graphics Card
  • red fire background

The light smoke adds the affect of a fire from the image of the pits of Hell

It was my first time using the image trace feature on Adobe Illustrator. While it isn't perfect it added an uncalled for aesthetic which in turn bends well with te text and background

Conclusion

Another fun and creative redesign project on a product that I've purchased. This project took a different approach to my usual design style, with a heavy focus on visual typography. In future redesign projects, I hope to improve my image quality when working with Adobe Dimension and learn more advanced Adobe Illustrator techniques to enhance my redesigns further.
