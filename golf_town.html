Research and development

As a current employee at Golf Town with over 2 years and counting of experience. I've seen what influences buying decisions and what marketing tactics work that get golfers into the store. I've done a SWOT analysis to gain a better understanding of the company's position in the market. In the analysis I was able to determine 2 areas of improvement.

Objectives & Goals

I used the SMART to describe and provide a clear, structured approach to setting my goals.

Primary Goal

Increase social media engagement by 50% on the YouTube channel.

Secondary Goal

Increase customer loyalty by 15%.

The Game Plan
  • Implement Loyalty program for all subsidiary stores. (Golf Town, Sporting Life, Team Town Sports)
  • Exclusive discounts and rewards will entice customers to return to us for all their golfing needs.
  • A loyalty program can help create a sense of belonging and community among Golf Town customers.
  • By providing personalized experiences and benefits, we can strengthen the customer's connection to Golf Town.
  • A well-designed loyalty program can differentiate Golf Town from competitors and attract new customers.

Strategy 1 - Increase social media engagement by 50% on the YouTube channel.

Description

To increase engagement on YouTube videos, we should start by producing more product reviews and golf video challenges. Follow and copy the trending golf content on YouTube and create more clickbait thumbnails.

Rationale

Trends & Audience Attraction

  • Younger demographics: Creating content similar to YouTube influencers like 'Good Good', 'H.I.T GOLF', or 'trottiegolf' can attract a younger audience.
  • Relatable content: Produce videos that relate with golfers, such as "$1000 BUDGET CLUB SET CHALLENGE!".

Product Reviews and Comparisons

  • Product reviews: Provide detailed information about products, including those used by tour professionals.
  • Product comparisons: Compare different golf equipment to help customers make informed decisions.
  • Establish credibility: Build trust by showcasing expertise and knowledge by golf town employees.

Driving Store Traffic and Sales

  • In-store visits: Motivate viewers to visit the store to try out products as seen from videos.
  • Generate leads: Attract potential customers to Golf Town through engaging content.
  • Build trust: Foster positive relationships with customers through transparent and informative content.
Tactics
  1. Change video content to be more product-driven to drive customers in stores.
  2. Change thumbnails to be eye-catching and clickable.
  3. Use other social media channels to promote new videos.
  4. Have in-store TVs display videos from the YouTube channel.
  5. Make videos that follow YouTube trends in golf space and relate to everyday golfers.
  6. Create short-form content to increase viewability.
Resources
  • Financial Budget: Depending on content of video(ex. on the course challenges green fees at golf courses.)
  • Equipment: Simulators, mics, cameras (all available at golf town stores).
Measurable Metrics
  • likes
  • Shares
  • Views
  • Youtube Analytics
  • Google Analytics

Strategy 2 - Increase brand loyalty by 15%.

Description

To increase brand loyalty, a points system could be implemented. The more a golfer shops at the store, the more points they will earn, which can be redeemed for discounts and other rewards.

Rationale
  • Follow market trends: Adopting strategies used by successful retailers like Loblaw or the Jim Pattison Group.
  • Implement a rewards program: Create a loyalty program similar to PC Optimum, More Rewards, or Scene +.
  • Offer exclusive benefits: Provide incentives for customers to shop exclusively at Golf Town and its subsidiaries stores.
  • Simplify the rewards process: Ensure customers can use a single account for all participating stores.
  • Attract more customers: Increase customer loyalty and drive sales by offering a competitive rewards program.
Tactics
  1. Deploy a points system that allows customers to accumulate points by shopping with Golf Town, which can be redeemed for discounts and other rewards.
  2. Offer a sign-up bonus to incentivize customers to join an download the app.
  3. Advertise the app on social media channels.
  4. Provide specific promotions within the app that increase the number of points earned on certain products (e.g., 10x points on a new lineup of drivers).
  5. Include a referral feature in the app, allowing users to earn additional points by referring friends.
  6. Invite golf influencers to Canada to play on top courses and promote the app.
Resources - Financial Budget
  • Points System Costs: Ensure that the points system is designed to avoid selling items below cost.
  • App Maintenance Costs: Budget for the expenses related to running and maintaining the app.
  • Influencer Costs: Allocate funds for hiring influencers to promote the app and cover their travel expenses.
Measurable Metrics
  • App downloads
  • User sign-ups from website
  • Friend referrals/li>
  • Website usage of accounts

Summary

This plan analyzes Golf Town's current state against its competitors and defines two business goals, along with associated strategies and implementation timelines.

The primary goal focuses on increasing social media engagement by 50% on the YouTube channel within 12 months due to manufacturing companies.

The secondary goal is to enhance customer loyalty by implementing a points rewards system.

Strategies are outlined with specific details on how to implement each plan efficiently, including social media marketing and competitor analysis.

Overall, the goals and strategies outlined will help Golf Town and its partners stay one step ahead of the competition.
