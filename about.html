My Values

Respect

My journey in martial arts began at a young age, where I was consistently taught the importance of self-respect and respecting others. Over six years of training, I earned my second-degree belt and learned that respect comes from within. Faced with different beliefs, I believe that everyone deserves to be treated with dignity. This understanding has shaped my approach to marketing strategy, where I prioritize building strong relationships based on mutual respect and understanding.

Excellence

My drive for excellence fuels everything I do. Whether I'm conquering challenging slopes on the ski hill or perfecting my golf game, I'm committed to pushing my limits and striving for mastery. I believe that the journey to success is often marked by mistakes, which I embrace as valuable learning opportunities. Through perseverance and a relentless pursuit of improvement, I've honed my skills and developed a strong foundation for achieving my goals.

Sustainability

A big passion of mine is minimizing my environmental impact. From diligently separating recyclables to actively cleaning up litter, I strive to make a positive difference for the animals most affected. Living in British Columbia, I'm fortunate to have access to abundant natural resources, which I believe it's our responsibility to protect. My goal is to reduce my carbon footprint and raise awareness about the harmful effects of everyday actions, such as releasing balloons into the air, littering, and idling cars to name a few.
